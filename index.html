Why Accessibility?

Good user experience (UX) starts with an accessible website. Additionally, 1 in 5 people have difficulty using the internet. We have an opportunity to improve the UX of the products we design and build with some easy approaches and techniques.

What is this page?

This is just a collection of sites, articles, tutorials and readings I have collected that have helped me build better websites. Someone asked me to help them learn more about accessibility, and the first thought was to share the links I reference. This is a living page, please suggest more, edit what I post and help me grow this into a great resource of resources.
